Click on a neighboring room (solid) to walk to the room. Charge the Wumpus by ctrl-clicking when you think you know where he is. Avoid corridors with monsters and rooms with bats. If a nearby corridor has monsters, the room is red, if a nearby room has bats, the cave is yellow. If a nearby room has the Wumpus, the room is orange. If the room is white, you haven't been there yet. If blue, everything is safe.
